Dummy Poem

In the realm of code, where languages thrive,
A symphony of syntax, diverse and alive.
Let me paint a poem with lines of code,
On programming languages, their purposes bestowed.

First, there's Python, a language of grace,
With elegance and simplicity, it sets the pace.
Its purpose, versatility, like a chameleon it adapts,
From web development to data science, it aptly maps.

Next, behold C++, a stalwart of might,
Efficiency and power, its essence takes flight.
Building complex systems, it does with flair,
From game engines to operating systems, it's everywhere.

Java steps forward, its motto: "write once, run anywhere,"
A platform independence it's proud to declare.
Web applets and enterprise software it spearheads,
Its object-oriented prowess, widely spreads.

In the realm of web, JavaScript shines bright,
A scripting language, woven into the HTML site.
Interactive web pages, it brings to life,
With its event-driven nature, it removes all strife.

For the data-driven world, we have SQL,
A language for databases, where records dwell.
Querying, managing, and manipulating data,
Its purpose, ensuring information stays in order.

Ruby appears, a jewel of elegance and joy,
Known for its simplicity, it's no ploy.
Web development and scripting, its forte,
With the Rails framework, it guides the way.

When it comes to statistical analysis and research,
R speaks volumes, leaving no lurch.
Data visualization and modeling, its might,
A language embraced by analysts day and night.

For the realm of mobile, Swift takes flight,
An Apple creation, precise and bright.
Crafting iOS and macOS apps with care,
It brings seamless experiences, beyond compare.

Last but not least, let's not forget HTML and CSS,
The backbone of the web, where layouts progress.
HTML, the structure, CSS, the style,
Together, they create web pages, versatile.

In this tapestry of languages, a universe of code,
Different purposes, different stories, they bestowed.
From software development to data's treasure trove,
Each language finds its purpose, a testament to growth.
